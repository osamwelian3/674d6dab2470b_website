MADIVA COMMUNITY BASED ORGANIZATION

OUR VISION

To eradicate gender based violence in our modern society.


OUR MISSION

To be a mirror in the society and champion for equal rights in our society, to have a platform where each other's voice matters, provide and created a conducive environment and empower youths, women and people living with disabilities by creating awareness on gender issues.


OUR MOTTO

End GBV, live a Peaceful Life.

Schools Outreach

MADIVA CBO undertaking sensitization of school girls from grade one to grade 8. End FGM creat a peaceful environment. A girl child should be sensitized on the consequences of FGM, medicalization of FGM should be stoped. We had a privilege to talk to the girls and their teachers. We agreed in unison that the practice with it's myths and misconceptions should be eradicated completely. Thanks to our partners International Solidarity Foundation-Kenya. We shall overcome.
